About The Journey

More than a competition. A season.

SOTY and AOTY aren't decided in a single vote. The journey spans months; from the earliest qualification contests in February, through a structured season of community voting, judge evaluations, and elimination stages, all the way to a winner reveal in late December.

This page is your front-row seat to the entire process. We document every stage, every announcement, and every milestone as SOTY & AOTY 2026 takes shape.

Whether you're a qualifier, a supporter, or just someone who loves great stories, the journey is for everyone.

Season Highlights

What makes this journey worth following.

Highlight 01

The Popularity Vote

Before the judging even begins, the community gets its say. The story with the most votes earns a fast-track directly into the Top 12, bypassing Stage 1 and 2.

2025: Only Pretend won the Popularity Vote
Highlight 02

The Authors Q&A Round

One of the most personal events of the season. AOTY qualifiers open up to the community answering questions about their craft, their stories, and their journey. It's where authors become more than a username.

2025: Eysenck stood out in this round
Highlight 03

Save-2-Stories

Stories are paired and voting for one saves both. It's one of the competition's most unique events, rewarding stories that build genuine community support rather than just individual votes.

2025: Artemis And The Aetherist & Painted Tension earned Fast Track
Highlight 04

The Head-to-Head Challenge

At the Top 12 stage, every vote counts exclusively. Supporting one story in a pair does nothing for the other. The 6 direct matchups that determine the Top 8 are always the most intense events of the season.

2025: Only Pretend took 1st in Head-to-Head
Highlight 05

Special Awards

Throughout the competition, our judging panel awards Best Directing, Best Plot, Best Interactivity, and Best Story Cover. Each award winner earns a Fast Track spot to advance a full stage, changing the competitive landscape.

2025: The Clique won Best Directing
Highlight 06

The Winner Reveal

The season always ends with a moment the community remembers. The SOTY and AOTY champions are crowned, special awards are announced, and both winners are permanently inducted into the Buddies Laureates Hall of Fame.

2025: Only Pretend · R. Lewis award champions
