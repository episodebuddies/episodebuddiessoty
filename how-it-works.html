Story of the Year

The competition that decides Episode's best story of the year.

SOTY is Buddies Support Hub's flagship annual competition. It identifies and celebrates the most outstanding story on Episode Interactive each season - evaluated not by popularity but by the quality of its craft, creativity, and impact on its readers.

Launched in 2022, SOTY uses a structured multi-stage judging process combining an expert panel with community voting, so the winner reflects both genuine quality and reader love.

Qualification

How stories earn their place

Only stories that have cleared a meaningful quality threshold enter SOTY. There are five official routes in.

Route 01
Win a Monthly Contest
Stories that win any official Buddies monthly contest earn automatic SOTY qualification. A win is already proof of a high standard.
Route 02
Score 90%+ in a Contest
Stories that score 90% or above in a monthly contest, even without winning also qualify. High craft scores are enough.
Route 03
Official Story Review
Authors can submit their story to the Buddies team for a formal review. Stories that meet the required benchmark are accepted.
Route 04
Team Nomination
The Buddies team actively watches the community and may nominate standout stories directly, no contest entry needed.
Route 05
Special Opportunity Slot
A direct application pathway for authors who missed other routes. Applications are reviewed and must meet the standard to be accepted.
Note
Read Cap
Stories with over 500,000 reads at the time the competition opens are not eligible, unless they qualified through a monthly contest.
Scoring Structure

How every story is scored

Every story's total is made up of two halves; a judge panel score and community voting, combined into a final score out of 100.

Judging Panel — 60%

Scored by 5 judges across Episodes 1–3

Directing20%
Scene composition, camera work, overlays, transitions
Plot & Writing20%
Hook, pacing, originality, character, dialogue, emotional impact
Interactivity10%
Reader choices, branching paths, tappables, customization
Story Cover10%
Visual quality, composition, relevance, originality
Judge Total 60 pts

Community Voting — 40%

Scored using a benchmark system across 4 events

Feed Vote15%
Instagram feed — likes, comments, shares
Poll Vote10%
Instagram Stories — 2 polls per story, groups of 4
Popularity Vote10%
Website vote — open to everyone, not just followers
Qualifier's Choice5%
Voted on by the competing authors themselves
Voting Total 40 pts
How voting scores are calculated (Benchmark Method): The story with the highest raw score in each voting category becomes the benchmark and earns the full points for that category. Every other story is scored proportionally. For example, if the benchmark is 900 votes and a story earns 756 — that's 756 ÷ 900 × 10 = 8.4 out of 10 for that category.
Voting Events

How votes are counted

Each voting event has its own point system. Here's exactly what earns points and how much.

Popularity Vote
10% of total score · Pre-competition

Held on the official Buddies website before the competition begins. All qualified stories are listed and the community votes freely for their favourite. Runs for approximately 7 days and is open to everyone, not just Instagram followers.

⚡ Fast Track: The story with the most votes earns automatic advancement to the Top 12, regardless of their cumulative score. All stories earn points counting toward their total.
Feed Vote
15% of total score

Each story is posted on the official Buddies Instagram feed. The community engages by liking, commenting, and sharing. Points are weighted by type and by whether the person is a follower of the page, comments carry the most weight, rewarding genuine, active interaction.

ActionFollowerNon-Follower
👍 Like4 points1 point
💬 Comment8 points2 points
🔁 Share3 points (any user)
Poll Vote
10% of total score

Runs through Instagram Stories. Stories are placed in groups of four and each story appears in three separate polls, giving every entry equal exposure to different audience segments. Voters pick their favourite from each group of four.

Vote TypePoints
Follower Vote8 points
Non-Follower Vote2 points
Qualifier's Choice Vote
5% of total score

Decided by the competing authors themselves; not the general public. Every qualifier votes for the story they feel stands out most based on quality, creativity, or impact, excluding their own entry. A smaller weight but a distinct kind of recognition, peer approval from people who've read the same stories.

Save-2-Stories (S2S) Vote
Stage 2 · Top 24

At Stage 2, all 24 remaining stories are paired into duos and posted on the Instagram feed. The unique mechanic: when a voter supports one story in a pair, their engagement is counted for both stories in that pair. This promotes shared visibility between authors at a critical stage.

⚡ Fast Track: The two pairs with the highest combined engagement advance directly to the Top 12, regardless of cumulative score. Combined with the Popularity Vote winner, this determines the 3 guaranteed Top 12 spots.
Head-to-Head (H2H) Challenge
Stage 3 · Top 12

The 12 remaining stories are placed into 6 direct pairs. Unlike S2S, this is exclusive - votes go to one story only and are not shared. Voting runs simultaneously through Instagram feed comments and/or Story polls.

Vote TypeFollowerNon-Follower
💬 Feed Comment8 points2 points
📊 Story Poll8 points2 points

Only one vote per person per pair is counted. H2H results add to each story's cumulative total and determine who advances to the Top 8.

Reader's Choice Vote
Recognition only · Does not affect score

A standalone award presented to the story that earned the highest vote total during the competition. The winner receives a special award but it does not contribute to any story's cumulative score.

Competition Stages

The road from qualifier to winner

SOTY runs from October through December across four progressive stages, each narrowing the field further.

Qualifier Announcements & Popularity Vote

Judges read Episodes 1–3 of every qualifying story and score across all four categories. Simultaneously, the Feed Vote, Poll Vote, and Qualifier's Choice Vote run publicly. Four Fast Track awards are decided by the judging panel and grant automatic Top 24 entry.

All 24 remaining stories carry their Stage 1 scores forward. The S2S Vote pairs stories into duos on the Instagram feed - engagement counts for both stories in a pair. The two pairs with the highest combined engagement fast-track to the Top 12. All other spots fill by cumulative score.
S2S Vote ⚡ Top 2 S2S pairs → Top 12 ⚡ Popularity Vote winner → Top 12

Top 12 → Top 8

The 12 semifinalists are placed into 6 direct matchups. Voting runs exclusively - no shared points. Feed comments and Story polls run simultaneously. H2H results are added to cumulative scores. Top 8 by overall total advance to the Final.

The Top 8 finalists compete in the final voting round. The Winner and all Runner Ups are announced. The SOTY Winner is inducted into the Buddies Laureates Hall of Fame and receives the full prize package including platform features and ongoing promotion.

Author of the Year

The competition that celebrates Episode's best author of the year.

AOTY is a lifetime honor. Where SOTY asks "is this a great story?" - AOTY asks "is this a great author?" It judges the person behind the work: their voice, presence, communication, and impact on the Episode community.

Once awarded, the title belongs to its winner for life. Past winners are permanently retired from the competition and inducted into the Buddies Laureates Hall of Fame.

Qualification

How authors enter

One rule, simply stated: To compete in AOTY, your story must first qualify for the current season's SOTY competition. Every SOTY qualifier is automatically eligible for AOTY. There is no separate application, no extra nomination, and no additional criteria to meet.

This also means AOTY draws from the same quality pool as SOTY — every competing author has already cleared a meaningful standard through the SOTY qualification process. The SOTY score from that same story also carries directly into AOTY scoring at 15%, so no extra reading is required.

Scoring Structure

How every author is scored

AOTY uses an equal 50/50 split between the judging panel and community voting, recognizing that both craft and community connection matter equally.

Judging Panel — 50%

Scored across 4 categories by the panel

Author's Interview20%
Q&A on writing journey, values, story, and vision; hosted by the Head Judge
SOTY Judging Score15%
Carried over directly from SOTY; no extra reading needed
Community Impact Message10%
Written message on the author's contribution to the Episode community
Instagram Aesthetics5%
Cohesion, quality, creativity, and professionalism of Instagram page
Profile Picture ★Award only
Judged for the Best Profile Picture award and Fast Track only, does not count toward score
Judge Total50 pts

Community Voting — 50%

Scored using a benchmark system across 4 events

Feed Vote15%
Instagram feed — likes, comments, shares
Poll Vote15%
Instagram Stories — 2 polls per author, groups of 4
Popularity Vote10%
Website vote — open to everyone
Author's Choice Vote10%
Voted on by all competing authors; not the public
Voting Total50 pts
How voting scores are calculated: The author with the highest raw score in each voting category is the benchmark and earns the full points. Everyone else is scaled proportionally. For example, if the benchmark is 822 poll votes and an author earns 658, that's 658 ÷ 822 × 15 = 12.01 out of 15 for the Poll Vote.

AOTY uses the same Feed Vote point system as SOTY. Here's a full breakdown of every event.

Held on the Buddies website before the competition begins. All 40 authors are listed. The community votes for their favourite over approximately 7 days.

⚡ Fast Track: The author with the most votes wins the Most Popular Author Award and Fast Tracks directly to the Top 12, bypassing Stage 1 and Stage 2 entirely.

15% of total score
