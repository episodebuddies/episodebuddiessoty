SOTY Feed Vote · Nov 8 – 14

Feed Vote Groups

8 stories posted per day over 5 days. Each group has a 24-hour voting window. Followers earn higher points than non-followers.

Points: Follower Like = 4 pts · Follower Comment = 8 pts · Any Share = 3 pts · Non-follower Like = 1 pt · Non-follower Comment = 2 pts. All votes count toward the story's cumulative Feed Vote score.

Poll Vote Groups

4 polls per day over 5 days — 20 polls total. Every story appears in exactly 2 different polls across the 5 days, always paired with different competitors.

How it works: Each poll shows 4 stories. Voters pick one. 80 total entries across 20 polls = 40 stories × 2 appearances. Follower vote = 8 pts · Non-follower vote = 2 pts.
