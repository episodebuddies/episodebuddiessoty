SOTY & AOTY 2026 — Official Rules & Guidelines

Buddies Support Hub · Issued for the 2026 Competition Season

Dear Participant, to ensure a fair, respectful, and uplifting experience for every author involved, the Buddies Support Hub has established the following official rules and guidelines. These standards govern the Story of the Year (SOTY) and Author of the Year (AOTY) competitions in their entirety, from the first submission to the final announcement.

These rules exist not to restrict creativity, but to protect it. They safeguard every participant's right to a fair, celebrated competition, and to an experience they can look back on with pride. We ask that you approach them with the same spirit of integrity and community that makes Buddies competitions something truly special.

Violations at any stage; including after results are announced may result in disqualification, bans, or revocation of titles. If you have any questions before participating, please contact us through the designated channels listed at the bottom of this document.

Rule 1 🤝

Respect & Conduct

The Buddies Support Hub was built on community. Everything you do; including how you compete must reflect the respect, kindness, and professionalism that this community represents. These standards apply from the moment you register until long after results are announced.

🚫
Respect for Organisers
Participants must treat all members of the Buddies Support Hub team with respect at all times. Harassment, rudeness, threatening behaviour, or public defamation of any team member will result in immediate disqualification.
Critical
🚫
Respect for Fellow Participants
Healthy, encouraging competition is welcome and celebrated. However, any form of disrespect, trash-talking, public mockery, or negative commentary directed at other participants in any space, public or private will not be tolerated.
Critical
★
Respect for Sponsors & Supporters
Participants must refrain from making negative, dismissive, or inflammatory comments about any sponsors, collaborators, or entities supporting the competitions. Their contributions are valued and essential to making this all possible.
Important
★
Conduct in Public Spaces
Your behaviour on Instagram, Threads, Discord, and any other platform where you are identifiable as a Buddies participant reflects on the community. Conduct unbecoming of the competition; even outside official Buddies spaces is subject to review.
Important
Rule 2 ✍️

Originality & Ownership

The SOTY competition celebrates original storytelling. Every entry must be a genuine expression of the author's creative vision. Shortcuts that undermine that authenticity; whether through AI generation, plagiarism, or misrepresentation of authorship are taken very seriously.

✓
Original Works Only
All stories submitted for SOTY must be original works created solely by the submitting author. Co-written stories are eligible only if both or all co-authors are listed and consent is confirmed.
Critical
✓
Rights Confirmation
By entering, authors confirm that they hold all necessary rights to their story, its visuals, overlays, character art, and all other content included. Using art, music, or written content without permission from the creator is prohibited.
Critical
🚫
No Plagiarism
Submitting another person's story as your own, or lifting substantial plot, dialogue, or directing elements from another work without credit, constitutes plagiarism. All credible reports will be thoroughly investigated and may result in immediate disqualification.
Critical
🚫
Restricted AI Use
The use of artificial intelligence for purposes beyond basic grammar or spell-checking is prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to: AI-generated written content, AI-generated artwork, and AI-produced backgrounds. Any AI involvement beyond proofreading is treated as a disqualifying factor.
Critical
Rule 3 ⚖️

Competition Integrity

The legitimacy of the SOTY and AOTY titles depends entirely on the integrity of the process. Any attempt to gain an unfair advantage; whether through influencing judges, vote manipulation, or any other means betrays every participant who competed fairly.

🚫
No Unfair Advantage
Participants must not engage in any actions that may be viewed as gaining an unfair advantage. This includes but is not limited to: attempting to influence, contact, or pressure judges regarding scores, forming vote-exchange agreements with other participants, or manipulating any competition metric.
Critical
★
Judge Decisions Are Final
The decisions of the judging panel are final and binding. Constructive feedback may be provided to participants after the competition, but disputes, score challenges, or demands for re-evaluation will not be entertained under any circumstances.
Critical
🚫
No Vote-Buying
Offering incentives - whether monetary, creative services, shoutouts, or any other form of exchange - in return for votes is strictly prohibited. This is considered one of the most serious integrity violations and will result in immediate disqualification.
Critical
!
Ethical Vote Campaigning
Public campaigning for votes is permitted within respectful limits. You may share your entry, ask your audience to vote, and promote your participation. However, spamming, mass DMs to strangers, vote-pressure tactics, or any form of manipulation constitute misconduct.
Important
Rule 4 🌟

Professional Behaviour

Participating in a Buddies competition carries a degree of responsibility. As part of one of the most celebrated author competitions in the Episode community, participants are expected to behave in ways that honour the Episode platform.

✓
Inclusive & Supportive Environment
Participants are expected to actively foster a supportive, inclusive environment throughout the competition. Celebrating others' achievements, cheering on fellow competitors, and uplifting the community are all encouraged.
Standard
🚫
No Alternate Accounts
The use of secondary, anonymous, or alternate accounts to vote, influence outcomes, or circumvent any competition rule is strictly forbidden. Any participant found using alternate accounts will face immediate disqualification from all competitions.
Critical
🚫
No Misinformation or Drama
Creating, spreading, or amplifying misinformation or drama about the competition, its structure, results, judges, participants, or the Buddies team is grounds for disqualification. This includes both direct statements and implied or suggestive content.
Critical
★
Private Complaint Process
All concerns, complaints, or suspected violations must be submitted privately to the Buddies Support Hub team through the designated contact channels. Public callouts, accusations, or venting sessions about competition issues, regardless of how valid the concern may be, are not the appropriate route and may be treated as misconduct.
Important
Rule 5 📝

Submission Guidelines

All submissions must follow the official guidelines precisely. Incomplete, incorrectly formatted, or late entries cannot be accepted - no exceptions. These requirements ensure a fair and consistent process for every participant.

★
Follow All Official Guidelines
Participants must follow all official submission guidelines in full, including formatting requirements, platform specifications, chapter requirements, and any category-specific instructions published for SOTY or AOTY.
Important
✓
One Entry Per Author (SOTY)
Only one story may be submitted per author for the Story of the Year competition. If multiple entries are received from the same author, only the earliest confirmed submission will be considered and all others will be withdrawn or the author will have to choose which one to keep.
Important
✓
Accurate AOTY Information
For the Author of the Year competition, all submitted profile information; including author name, Instagram handle, story links, and personal statements - must be accurate, truthful, and representative of the author's genuine work and identity.
Important
🚫
Strict Deadlines
Submissions received after the published deadline will not be considered, reviewed, or added to the competition. No extensions will be granted. We strongly recommend submitting at least 24 hours before any deadline to account for technical issues.
Critical
★
Story Must Be Published & Accessible
SOTY entries must be publicly accessible on Episode at the time of submission and throughout the entire competition period. Stories that are removed, hidden, or made inaccessible after submission will be withdrawn from consideration.
Important
Rule 6 📢

Publicity & Promotion

The Buddies competitions are a celebration, and we want to celebrate you. By participating, you grant us the ability to share your story and achievements; but we always do so with care and respect for your work.

✓
Promotional Rights Granted
By participating, authors grant Buddies Support Hub the non-exclusive right to use their name, likeness, Instagram handle, and submitted work (or excerpts thereof) for promotional purposes across social media platforms, the Buddies website, and related publications. This right is limited to competition-related promotion only.
Standard
✓
Post-Competition Activities
Winners and finalists may be invited to participate in post-event publicity activities; including interviews, features, Q&A sessions, and spotlights - coordinated by the Buddies Support Hub. Participation in these activities is encouraged but not compulsory.
Standard
★
Author Credit Always Given
Buddies Support Hub will always credit the author when sharing their work, profile, or story excerpts. We will never misrepresent, alter, or use your work in a manner that could be considered damaging to your reputation or creative integrity.
Standard
Rule 7 🛡️

Content Standards

Buddies competitions are open to the entire Episode community, including younger readers. All submitted content; stories, profiles, promotional materials, and public communications - must meet the content standards outlined below.

🚫
No Inappropriate Content
The presence of sensitive or inappropriate content; including but not limited to explicit sexual material, graphic violence, gore, or hate speech that may not have been identified or filtered by Episode's review team is not permitted in any SOTY submission.
Critical
🚫
No Discriminatory Content
Stories or public communications that promote, normalise, or celebrate discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other protected characteristic will be disqualified. Inclusive storytelling is a Buddies value.
Critical
!
Sensitive Topics — Handled with Care
Stories that explore sensitive themes (mental health, trauma, abuse, grief) are welcome and valued, provided they are handled responsibly and with appropriate context. The Buddies team reserves the right to flag entries that approach sensitive topics in a manner deemed harmful or exploitative.
Important
Rule 8 🏅

Eligibility Requirements

To maintain the fairness and prestige of the competitions, all participants must meet the following eligibility requirements at the time of entry and throughout the competition period.

✓
Active Episode Author
All SOTY participants must have a published story on Episode Interactive at the time of submission. All AOTY participants must be active authors within the Episode community with a verifiable presence and published stories.
Important
✓
Community Qualification
AOTY participants must have qualified through the official Buddies monthly contest pathway or another officially designated qualification route for the 2026 season. Self-nomination outside of official channels will not be accepted.
Important
🚫
Good Standing Required
Participants who have been previously disqualified or banned from any Buddies competition are not eligible to re-enter unless an official reinstatement has been granted by the Buddies team. Prior violations are taken into account during eligibility review.
Important
★
No Current Buddies Team Members
Active members of the Buddies Support Hub organising team and judging panel are not eligible to participate as competitors in either SOTY or AOTY during seasons in which they hold an active team role. This includes all planning committee members.
Standard
Rule 9 ⚠️

Consequences for Misconduct

Violations of these rules are taken seriously and handled with care, consistency, and fairness. The Buddies team investigates all credible reports before acting. Consequences are applied proportionally to the severity of the violation.

⚠️
Tier 1 — Warning
Issued for first-time or minor violations. The participant is notified privately and given the opportunity to correct their conduct. Repeated minor violations escalate to Tier 2.
🚫
Tier 2 — Disqualification
Applied for serious or repeated violations. The participant is removed from the current competition. Disqualification may be immediate for critical violations such as vote-buying, plagiarism, or judge interference.
🔒
Tier 3 — Permanent Ban
Reserved for the most severe violations or repeated misconduct across multiple seasons. The participant is permanently banned from all future Buddies competitions and events. All existing titles may also be revoked.
All violations may also result in
!
Revocation of Awards & Titles
Awards, badges, titles, and recognition; including the SOTY and AOTY titles, may be revoked even after the competition has ended if a violation is discovered retrospectively. The runner-up or next qualifying participant may be elevated in their place.
Critical
!
Removal from Hall of Fame
In cases of serious misconduct, a participant's name and record may be removed from the Buddies Hall of Fame and all historical competition records maintained by the Hub.
Critical
Rule 10 ✅

Acknowledgement & Agreement

These rules are not designed to intimidate, they are designed to protect every participant equally. By entering the competition, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to abide by all rules set forth by the Buddies Support Hub.

✓
Full Agreement Required
Participation in any Buddies competition constitutes full agreement with these guidelines. There are no partial acceptances; entering means you agree to every rule in this document in its entirety.
Critical
★
Rules May Be Updated
The Buddies Support Hub reserves the right to update or amend these guidelines before the competition season begins. Participants will be notified of any significant changes. The most current version of this document always takes precedence.
Standard
✓
Questions Welcome
If any rule is unclear or you have a question before registering, please contact the Buddies team privately at episodebuddies3@gmail.com or through the designated Instagram DM. We are always happy to clarify.
Standard
