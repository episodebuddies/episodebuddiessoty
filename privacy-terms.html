Privacy & Terms

How we handle your information and the rules that govern use of this site. We keep things simple; this is a community-run platform, not a corporation.

Privacy Policy Last updated: May 2026
Section 01

Introduction

Welcome to the Buddies Support Hub SOTY & AOTY website. This Privacy Policy explains how the Buddies Team ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit or interact with our site.

We are a community-run, volunteer-operated platform dedicated to celebrating Episode authors. We are not a corporation, and we handle your information with care and transparency. By using this site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

A note from the Buddies Team: We collect only what is necessary to run our competitions and community services. We do not sell, trade, or monetise your data in any way.
Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: information you voluntarily provide to us, and limited technical data generated automatically by your device.

2.1 Form Submissions

When you submit a contact form, competition registration form, or any other form on this site, we may collect the following:

  • Name - your display name or author name as provided by you.
  • Email address - used to contact you regarding your submission or competition entry.
  • Instagram handle - collected through registration and contact forms to identify your community presence and link to your author profile where applicable.
  • Any other details you voluntarily include in a message or submission field.

Submission of this information is always optional and voluntary. You are not required to submit any form to browse the site.

2.2 Usage Data (Local Storage)

Some features of this site use your browser's localStorage to remember your preferences - for example, which competition panel you last viewed. This data is stored entirely on your own device and is never transmitted to us or any third party. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

We do not use tracking cookies, fingerprinting, or any invasive analytics technology.

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • To process and manage competition entries and registrations.
  • To contact you with updates, results, or important announcements related to a competition you have entered.
  • To display winner profiles and achievements on this site, using publicly agreed-upon information such as your author name and Instagram handle.
  • To respond to enquiries submitted through our contact form.
  • To improve the experience and content of this site based on general usage patterns.

We do not use your information for automated decision-making, profiling, or marketing. We will never share your personal information with third parties for their own commercial purposes.

Information shared with the team is only accessible to authorized members when necessary for moderation, organization, or event management purposes.

Any information shared by participants (such as story titles, covers, usernames, or promotional materials) may be reposted or featured on our official community platforms for promotional or recognition purposes.

By submitting information to the team, you acknowledge and agree to the use of your information as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Section 04

Cookies and Local Storage

This site does not use cookies to track or identify you. We use browser localStorage only to store non-personal, session-level preferences on your own device (such as your chosen panel view). No personal data is stored in localStorage.

Third-party services embedded in or linked from this site may set their own cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies. Please refer to Section 5 for details on third-party services we use.

Your control: You can disable or clear localStorage through your browser settings at any time without affecting the core functionality of this site.
Section 05

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this site. Each has its own privacy policy and data practices, which we encourage you to review:

Formspree Google Sheets Cloudinary Instagram Vercel
  • Formspree - processes form submissions on our behalf. When you submit a form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers and forwarded to us via email. Formspree's privacy policy governs how they handle that data in transit.
  • Google Sheets - used internally by the Buddies Team to log and manage competition entries and votes. Data entered into our forms may be stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to team administrators.
  • Cloudinary - hosts images displayed on this site (story covers, author profile pictures, logos). No personal visitor data is shared with Cloudinary.
  • Instagram links - author profile links direct you to Instagram. Clicking these links takes you to a third-party platform governed by Instagram's own privacy policy. We do not share your data with Instagram.
  • Vercel - hosts this website and may collect standard server access logs (IP address, request time, page visited) for performance and security purposes, in line with Vercel's privacy policy.
Section 06

Your Rights

As someone who interacts with our site, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access - you may request a copy of any personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction - you may ask us to correct any inaccurate information we have on record.
  • Deletion - you may request that we delete your personal information from our records. Please note that winner profiles are part of our permanent archive; names and handles published as part of competition results are considered public record and may not be fully removable.
  • Withdrawal of consent - where you have provided information voluntarily, you may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us.
  • Data portability - you may request a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 8.

Section 07

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as it is necessary for the purpose for which it was collected. Specifically:

  • Competition entry data is retained for the duration of the relevant competition season and for a reasonable administrative period afterward.
  • Contact form messages are retained only for as long as needed to address your enquiry.
  • Winner profiles (name, Instagram handle, and publicly agreed-upon information) are retained permanently as part of our historical archive, in keeping with the purpose of this site.
  • localStorage data is retained on your device until you clear it; we have no access to or control over this data.

If you wish to request early deletion of your data, please contact us as set out in Section 8.

Section 08

Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests relating to this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please reach out to the Buddies Team directly:
