What Qualifying Means

You've already earned your place.

Qualifying for SOTY is not automatic. Every story in this competition has cleared a meaningful quality threshold; through a contest win, a high score, a review, a team nomination, or a Special Opportunity Slot. Being here already sets you apart.

As a SOTY qualifier you are also automatically eligible for AOTY. No separate application needed. Both competitions run simultaneously across the same season.

Two competitions. One qualification.
SOTY asks: Is this a great story?  ·  AOTY asks: Is this a great author?
Your story is judged in SOTY. You - your voice, presence, and community impact are judged in AOTY.

Qualification Routes

How you earned your spot

There are five official pathways into SOTY. Whichever route brought you here, it reflects a demonstrated standard of quality.

Route 01
Monthly Contest Win
You won an official Buddies monthly contest. Your story has already been evaluated against defined criteria and cleared a high threshold.
Route 02
Score of 75%+
You scored 75 or above in a monthly contest even without winning. That score is not given lightly, and it earned you a direct entry.
Route 03
Official Story Review
Your story was submitted for a formal Buddies review outside of contest cycles and met the required SOTY benchmark.
Route 04
Team Nomination
The Buddies team spotted your work and nominated it directly. No application required, you were identified as genuinely outstanding.
Route 05
Special Opportunity Slot
You applied directly and your story was reviewed and accepted. Entry through this route is earned, not given - it carries equal weight to every other path.
Note
AOTY Eligibility
Your SOTY qualification automatically makes you eligible for AOTY. Past AOTY winners; Fern (2023), Ryan Graye (2024), R. Lewis (2025), cannot re-enter AOTY and SOTY.

What to Prepare

Your pre-competition checklist

Before the season officially opens, here is what you should have ready for each competition.

Judges read Episodes 1, 2, and 3 only. Everything scored is based on those three episodes. Make sure they represent your best work before the season opens.
✦
Polish Episodes 1–3
Your first three episodes are everything in SOTY. Review your directing, writing, choices, and cover before the competition begins. While you may continue editing your story during the competition, judges read and score entries at their own pace; once a judge has read your story, their score reflects what they saw at that moment. There is no way to know when your story will be read.
Most important
🎬
Review your Story Cover
Your cover is worth 10% of your judge score and determines the Best Story Cover Award. It should be high quality, visually striking, and immediately communicate your story's tone and genre.
10% of judge score
📣
Brief your audience ahead of voting
Community voting makes up 40% of your total score. Let your readers know the competition is coming so they're ready to support you when the Feed Vote, Poll Vote, and Popularity Vote open.
Community voting: 40%
🗳️
Vote in the Popularity Vote
The Popularity Vote opens on the Buddies website before the competition begins. As a qualifier, you will also cast a Qualifier's Choice Vote for the story you believe stands out most - excluding your own. This contributes 5% to every qualifying story's final score.
📋
Complete your Registration Form
Your story details, Episode link, and qualifying route must be confirmed via the official submission form. Make sure everything is accurate, this is the information the team and judges will use throughout the competition.
Required
AOTY asks a different question: not just what you've written, but who you are as an author. Every submission is a reflection of your voice, your character, and your place in this community. Make it count.
📝
Prepare your Community Impact Message
Your Community Impact Message is submitted during registration. It is written around a theme of your choice. Pick the one that best reflects who you are and write sincerely within it. This is worth 10% of your final score.
Submit at registration · 10% of score
🎤
Prepare for your Author's Interview
The Interview is hosted by the Judges on Instagram and is worth 20% of your total score; the largest single judge category in AOTY. You'll answer questions about your writing journey, your story, and why you believe you should win. Come prepared, be yourself, and make it count.
20% of score · Most important
📷
Clean up your Instagram profile
Judges will visit your Instagram page as part of the evaluation. Your Instagram profile is part of your AOTY presence. Make sure it reflects who you are as an author. The Best PFP Award and Best IG Aesthetics Award both carry Fast Track spots into the Top 24.
Fast Track awards available
📣
Brief your audience ahead of voting
Community voting is 50% of your AOTY total, equal to the entire judge half. The Feed Vote (15%), Poll Vote (15%), Popularity Vote (10%), and Author's Choice Vote (10%) all require your readers and fellow authors to show up for you.
Community voting: 50%
📋
Complete your Registration Form
Your registration form confirms your AOTY entry and is where you submit your Community Impact Message. Ensure all your details, Instagram handle, Episode profile, story link - are accurate before submitting.
Required · Includes Impact Message
🗳️
Vote in the Author's Choice Vote
Every competing author casts a vote for the author they believe stands out most; anyone but themselves. Your vote contributes to another author's 10% Author's Choice score. Participate honestly and thoughtfully, it's a mark of respect for the craft.

How voting scores work (Benchmark Method): The story or author with the highest raw votes in each event becomes the benchmark and earns the full points for that category. Everyone else is scored proportionally. For example, if the benchmark Feed Vote total is 900 points and you earn 756 - that's 756 ÷ 900 × 15 = 12.6 out of 15.

Voting Events

How every vote is counted

Each voting event has its own point system. Here is exactly what earns points, how much, and what you need to know about each one.

Feed Vote
SOTY 15% · AOTY 15%

Each story or author is posted on the official Buddies Instagram feed. The community supports by liking, commenting, and sharing. Comments carry the highest point value; genuine, active engagement is rewarded over passive likes.

ActionFollowerNon-Follower
👍 Like4 points1 point
💬 Comment8 points2 points
🔁 Share3 points (any user)
Poll Vote
SOTY 10% · AOTY 15%

Runs through Instagram Stories. Stories and authors are placed in groups of four. Each entry appears in two separate polls across the voting period, ensuring equal exposure to different audience segments regardless of when followers check Stories.

Vote TypePoints
Follower Vote8 points
Non-Follower Vote2 points
Popularity Vote
SOTY 10% · AOTY 10% · Pre-competition

Held on the official Buddies website before the competition formally begins. All qualifiers are listed and the community votes freely, open to everyone, not just Instagram followers. Runs for approximately 7 days.

⚡ Fast Track: The story/author with the most votes earns automatic advancement; SOTY winner goes to the Top 12, AOTY winner (Most Popular Author) goes directly to the Top 12, bypassing Stages 1 and 2 entirely. All qualifiers earn points toward their cumulative total.
Qualifier's Choice / Author's Choice Vote
SOTY 5% · AOTY 10%

Decided entirely by the competing qualifiers, not the general public. Every qualifier votes for the story or author they believe stands out most based on quality, creativity, or impact, excluding their own entry. A smaller weighting, but a distinct kind of recognition, votes from people who have read the same stories and love the craft.

Save-2-Stories (S2S) Vote
SOTY only · Stage 2 · Top 24

All 24 remaining SOTY stories are paired into duos and posted on the Instagram feed. The unique mechanic: when a voter engages with one story in a pair, their engagement counts for both stories in that pair. This is a collaborative moment, you and your pair partner benefit from each other's audiences.

⚡ Fast Track: The two pairs with the highest combined engagement advance directly to the Top 12, regardless of cumulative score.
Head-to-Head (H2H) Challenge
Stage 3 · Top 12

The 12 semifinalists are placed in 6 direct matchups. Unlike S2S, this is exclusive, votes go to one entry only. Voting runs through Instagram feed comments only. For AOTY, each author also answers a question relating to Episode trends. Combined H2H results add to cumulative scores.

Vote TypeFollowerNon-Follower
💬 Feed Comment8 points2 points

Only one vote per person is counted.

For Community Voters

Voting rules & guidelines

Community voting is open to everyone, but there are rules that keep the competition fair. Please read these before casting any votes.

Instagram voting covers two events: the Poll Vote (Story polls) and the Feed Vote (comments, likes, and shares on posts). Both run through the official Buddies Instagram page.
Story Poll Voting
📊
Open to all Instagram users
Voting through Instagram Story polls is open to all Instagram users. No requirements beyond having an account.
👤
One vote per poll, per account
Each Instagram account may cast one vote per poll. Instagram enforces this natively, only one response per poll per account is recorded.
✅
Official poll results are final
The final poll results displayed by Instagram at the close of voting will be used as the official count and weighted accordingly.
Feed Voting
📌
Voting takes place on designated posts
Feed voting will be conducted through designated voting posts published on the official Buddies Instagram page.
🏷️
Comments must include the official hashtag
To cast a valid comment vote, participants must leave a comment containing the official voting hashtag - #buddies_soty2026 for SOTY voting and #buddies_aoty2026 for AOTY voting. Only comments containing the correct hashtag will be counted.
👍
Likes and shares also count
Votes can be cast through likes, comments (with the correct hashtag), and shares. Each type of engagement contributes to the vote total.
💬
Multiple comments count as one vote
Multiple comments from the same account on the same voting post will be counted as a single vote.
Fair Voting
🚫
No fake accounts, bots, or manipulation
The use of fake accounts, bots, automated engagement tools, or other forms of vote manipulation is prohibited. Votes identified as fraudulent or suspicious may be removed during verification.
The Popularity Vote runs on the official Buddies website and is open to everyone, no Instagram account required. It contributes 10% to the overall community voting score for both SOTY and AOTY.
Eligibility
🌐
Open during the official voting period
Website voting is open throughout the official popularity voting period. Votes must be submitted through the official SOTY & AOTY voting platform.
Voting Limit
🗳️
One vote per person, per round
Each participant may vote once per voting round unless otherwise specified. Duplicate or suspicious votes may be removed during the verification process.
Vote Verification
🔍
All votes are subject to review
All website votes are subject to review and verification before final results are published. Votes that violate voting guidelines or are determined to be fraudulent will be invalidated.
Voting Period
⏱️
Only votes within the window are counted
Only votes submitted during the official voting period will be counted. Check the official Buddies page for announced open and close dates.
Popularity Award Weight
⚡
Contributes to the Popularity Award standings
Website voting contributes to the Popularity Award standings. The Popularity Award represents 10% of the overall community voting score used within the SOTY & AOTY competition structure.
Final Decisions
⚖️
Buddies team' decisions are final
We reserve the right to interpret and enforce these rules to protect the integrity of the voting process. All decisions regarding vote verification and final results are final.

Competition Rules

What you need to know and follow

The competition has a small number of conditions that can result in disqualification. Read these carefully; if you ever have a concern, contact the team immediately rather than acting independently.

🚫
Plagiarism
Any confirmed case of copying another author's work; story premises, dialogue, directing overlays, or visual assets - results in immediate disqualification from SOTY and AOTY.
🚫
Episode Guideline Violations
Stories that violate Episode Interactive's official platform guidelines will be flagged for review and may be removed. This applies across both competitions.
🚫
Exceeding the Read Cap (SOTY)
Stories with more than 500,000 reads at the time the competition opens (except stories that qualified through monthly contest) are not eligible for SOTY. This cap exists to level the competition field.
🚫
Prior AOTY Win
Past AOTY winners cannot re-enter both competitions under any circumstances. Past winners: Fern (2023), Ryan Graye (2024), R. Lewis (2025).
↩️
SOTY Disqualification (AOTY knock-on)
If your SOTY entry is disqualified after AOTY has begun, your AOTY entry is also removed, since SOTY qualification is the basis for AOTY eligibility.
🤝
Voluntary Withdrawal
You may withdraw before the competition begins by notifying the Buddies team. Withdrawn entries are removed from all scoring and do not receive a placement. Contact the team privately.
If you notice a potential issue, report it. Judges handle all eligibility decisions. If you believe something may violate the rules at any point during the competition, contact the Buddies team directly at episodebuddies3@gmail.com as soon as possible.

Qualifier conduct during the competition

01
Do not approach judges about your scores or standing
Judges are not permitted to discuss evaluations, scores, or your chances of advancing during the active competition period. If a judge reaches out to you, respond normally, but do not ask about your evaluation.
02
Vote honestly in the Qualifier's / Author's Choice Vote
These Qualifiers' choice votes are decided by you and your fellow authors. Vote for the story or author you genuinely believe stands out most - excluding your own entry. Your honesty is the integrity of this vote.
03
Do not attempt to influence judging outcomes
Coordinating with judges, offering incentives, or attempting to find out scoring information before the official announcement is a breach of competition integrity and may result in disqualification.
04
Keep your story accessible throughout the competition
Judges must be able to read your first three episodes at any point during the judging window. Do not remove or restrict your story after the competition begins.
05
When in doubt, contact the team
Any question about eligibility, conduct, or the rules should go directly to the Buddies team. Do not make independent decisions about grey-area situations, always ask first.
