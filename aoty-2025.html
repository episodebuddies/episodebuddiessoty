Entry & Structure

To qualify for AOTY, your story must be entered into the 2025 Story of the Year competition. Every SOTY qualifier is automatically eligible to compete in AOTY — no separate application is needed.

AOTY evaluates the author as a whole — their Q&A responses, Instagram presence, community impact, and dedication — not just the story. The title is a lifetime honour; past winners cannot re-enter.

All 40 authors enter an online website poll. The public votes for their favourite author. The author with the most votes automatically advances to the Top 12, skipping all earlier stages.

Stage 1 runs four events simultaneously. The Instagram Feed Vote (authors in groups of 4, scored by likes/comments/shares) and Instagram Poll Vote (same groups, Stories) determine community popularity. The Q&A Round has authors answer curated questions scored by the judging panel on clarity, authenticity, impact, creativity, and relevance. The Best Aesthetics Contest has judges evaluate each author's Instagram profile cohesion and visual branding.

The Top 24 are announced — 20 from Stage 1 scores plus 4 Fast Track spots (Best PFP, Most Devoted Author, Best Aesthetics, Best Q&A). A 2nd Poll Vote runs to determine which 12 advance, alongside the Most Popular Author vote which grants an additional Fast Track to the Top 12.

The Top 12 are announced. Authors are paired into 6 head-to-head matchups. Each author writes and shares an impactful message for their fans and the Episode community. The community votes via comments on the feed post, and the judging panel separately scores each message on impact, clarity, originality, and community value. Combined scores determine which 8 advance.

The Top 8 are announced. Each remaining author answers one final question in a private interview. Scores from the final interview are added to cumulative totals. A final poll vote runs. The Top 4 are revealed before the overall winner and all special award winners are announced.
