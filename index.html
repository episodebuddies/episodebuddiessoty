The Competitions

Two competitions. One community.

SOTY and AOTY run together every October–December, asking the same community two very different questions.

Story of the Year
Is this a great story?

SOTY is our flagship annual competition, dedicated to identifying and honoring the single most outstanding story published on Episode each season. Entries are judged not by popularity, but by craft; plot, directing, interactivity, and visual design.

Judging: 60% judge panel · 40% community voting
Rubric: Plot & Writing · Directing · Interactivity · Story Cover
Read cap: Stories must have 250,000 reads or fewer
Title: Can be won more than once, not a lifetime award
Season: Competition runs October – December annually
Author of the Year
Is this a great author?

AOTY judges the author as a whole; their Q&A responses, Instagram presence, community impact, and dedication to their craft. Every SOTY qualifier is automatically eligible. The title is held for life; past winners are permanently retired.

Judging: 50% judge panel · 50% community voting
Rubric: Q&A Round · Instagram Aesthetics · Story Evaluation
Field: 40 authors per season
Title: Lifetime honour, past winners cannot re-enter
Eligibility: Automatic entry for all SOTY qualifiers
