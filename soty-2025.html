Entry & Structure

To qualify for SOTY, a story must earn its place through one of the following pathways:

Enter a monthly Buddies contest and receive a score of 90 or higher.
Apply for a story review and receive a score of 90 or higher.
Receive a Team Nomination from any member of the Buddies team.
Apply through the Special Opportunity Slot when applications open.
Receive an Honorable Mention from any Buddies team member.
The 400,000 read cap applies; stories exceeding this limit at the time of the competition opening are not eligible to compete.

Before the contest begins, all qualified stories enter an online vote on the official Buddies website. The story with the most votes automatically advances to the semifinals (Top 12), regardless of their Stage 1 score.

Stage 1 includes four events: the Judges' Evaluation (4 categories, Directing, Interactivity, Plot, Cover), the 1st Feed Vote (40 stories in groups of 4, scored by likes/comments/shares), the 1st Poll Vote (same groups, Instagram Stories), and the Author's Vote (each contestant votes for 3 other stories, ranked 1st/2nd/3rd). Top performers in each event earn points toward the Top 24.

The 20 surviving stories plus 4 Fast Track award winners are publicly announced as the Top 24. Stage 2 includes the Save-2-Stories (S2S) Vote: stories are paired in duos and a vote for one counts for both. The two pairs with the highest engagement earn Fast Track spots to the Top 12. A 2nd Poll Vote also run during this stage.

The Top 12 are announced, then placed into 6 head-to-head pairs. Votes are exclusive, a vote for one story does NOT count for the other. Voting happens via Instagram feed comments and Instagram Story polls simultaneously. Combined scores determine which 8 stories advance.

The Top 8 are announced. A Final Feed Vote runs for all remaining stories. Authors share a message with their fans. The Top 4 are then announced, followed by the final winner announcement and all special award reveals. The winner is inducted into the Buddies Laureates Hall of Fame.
