Qualifying 6

Qualifying for SOTY starts with writing a story that keeps readers engaged. Once you have that, there are four routes in:

  • Monthly Contest Score: Enter any Buddies monthly contest and score 90 or above. Contest winners also automatically qualify.
  • Story Review: Submit your story for an official review by the Buddies team. Stories that meet the required standard of quality, creativity, and impact qualify.
  • Team Nomination: Our team evaluates standout authors throughout the year. If your work has made a strong impression, you may be nominated directly.
  • Special Opportunity Slot: Introduced in 2025, this gives authors a direct application route even if they missed other opportunities.

Once you qualify for SOTY, you are automatically eligible for AOTY; no separate application needed.

This rule exists to give lesser-known stories a fair chance against more established ones, and to keep the competition focused on discovering hidden gems rather than crowning already popular stories.

The only restriction is that a story cannot re-enter. The author, however, can always come back with a new project.

Qualification for SOTY opens in February each year when the first monthly contest of the season launches and closes at the end of September. The competition season itself then runs from October through December.

Special Opportunity Slot applications open at a separate time during the year and are announced on our Instagram page when they become available.

Your story does not need to be fully completed to qualify or compete in SOTY. However, it must have at least the minimum number of episodes required for the qualifying method you are using; contest entries and reviews both evaluate the first three episodes as a baseline.

During the competition itself, judges and voters assess what is available, so the more polished and complete your published chapters are, the stronger your standing will be.

Story of the Year — SOTY 6

SOTY is about a single story. Judges and the community evaluate the story itself, its plot, directing, interactivity, and visual design. If your story wins SOTY but you don't take home the AOTY award, you can still enter a future season with a brand-new project.

AOTY is about the author as a whole, their Q&A responses, Instagram presence, community impact, and dedication to their craft. The AOTY title is a lifetime honour, meaning past winners cannot re-enter in future seasons.

Both competitions run simultaneously during October–December, and every SOTY qualifier is automatically eligible for AOTY.

SOTY runs across four main stages after a pre-competition Popularity Vote:

  • Stage 1 (38 → 24): Author's Interview, 1st Feed Vote, 1st Poll Vote, and Author's Vote all run simultaneously.
  • Stage 2 (24 → 12): Save-2-Stories (S2S) Vote; stories compete in pairs, and a vote for one counts for both.
  • Stage 3 / Semifinals (12 → 8): Head-to-Head Challenge; exclusive pairs, where votes are not shared.
  • Stage 4 / Finals (8 → Winner): Final Feed Vote, Author's Messages, and Winner Announcement.

Fast Track spots from special award winners allow some stories to skip ahead to the Top 24 or Top 12 without going through earlier voting stages.

Fast Track spots allow stories or authors to skip directly to a later stage of the competition based on winning a special award earlier in the season.

For SOTY, winning Best Story Cover, Best Directing, Best Interactivity, or Best Plot earns a story a direct spot in the Top 24. Winning the Most Popular Story award or the S2S Vote earns a direct spot in the Top 12.

Fast Tracked stories still participate in remaining voting events, they just don't have to fight through earlier elimination rounds to get there.

Yes - but not with the same story. A winning story is permanently retired from future competitions. However, the author behind it is free to qualify again with an entirely new story in a later season. This is intentional, it ensures SOTY continually discovers fresh narratives rather than cycling the same titles.

This is one key difference from AOTY, where a win permanently retires the author from future competition regardless of what they write next.

SOTY judges evaluate stories across four main areas:

  • Plot & Writing: Narrative structure, originality, pacing, emotional depth, and character development.
  • Directing: Scene composition, camera work, overlays, transitions, and visual creativity.
  • Interactivity: Quality of choices, branching, tappables, and use of Episode's interactive features.
  • Story Cover: Visual impact, originality, and relevance of the cover design.

Judge scores account for 60% of a story's final result. Community voting accounts for the remaining 40%.

SOTY launched in 2022 as the Buddies Support Hub's first major annual competition. The inaugural winner was Boarding Incomplete by Nadi. Since then, a new Story of the Year has been crowned every December, making 2026 the 5th season of the competition.

Each season has grown in scale, with more entries, more voting events, and a more structured judging system than the year before.

Author of the Year — AOTY 5

No. Once an author wins Author of the Year, they cannot compete again in any future AOTY season. AOTY is a lifetime title; think of it as being permanently inducted into the Buddies Laureates Hall of Fame.

Winning AOTY comes with exclusive ongoing perks: continued author promotion, spotlighting across our platforms, and a permanent place in Buddies history. The retirement rule exists to ensure the title always goes to someone new, and to keep the legacy of Laureates growing year after year.

Past AOTY winners: Fern (2023) · Ryan Graye (2024) · R. Lewis (2025). None of them can re-enter.

The Q&A Round is one of AOTY's most important events. Each author answers a curated set of questions about their writing journey, their SOTY story, personal values, and why they believe they should win AOTY.

Responses are judged privately by the selection committee, there is no public voting in this round. Judges score each author on five criteria:

  • Clarity & Expression: How clearly and fluently the author communicates.
  • Authenticity: Genuine, personal, and sincere reflections rather than rehearsed answers.
  • Impact & Vision: How the author plans to inspire or contribute to the community.
  • Creativity: Unique perspectives and engaging storytelling even in their responses.
  • Relevance: Staying on topic and fully addressing the questions asked.

Yes - significantly. AOTY evaluates the author, not just the story. Your Instagram presence is assessed through two specific awards: Best Profile Picture (judged by the committee and the community) and Best Instagram Aesthetics (judged by the committee based on your profile's visual cohesion, consistency, and branding).

Winning either of these earns a Fast Track spot into the Top 24. So a polished, well-maintained Instagram feed can give you a meaningful head start before the main voting rounds even begin.

Beyond awards, community voting throughout AOTY rewards authors who have an active, engaged following; so maintaining a strong Instagram presence throughout the qualification window matters.

AOTY uses a 50/50 split between judge panel scores and community voting, giving the audience equal weight to the selection committee in determining the winner. This is different from SOTY, which uses a 60/40 split in favour of judges.

The equal split reflects AOTY's community-first philosophy; the author who wins should be someone the entire Episode community recognizes and celebrates, not just someone who scored well behind closed doors.

Alongside the main AOTY title, six Special Honours are awarded each season to recognize contributions that go beyond a single competition result:

  • Rising Star Award: A new or emerging author whose debut story shows exceptional potential.
  • Lifetime Achievement Award: A long-standing author whose years of contribution have shaped the community.
  • Community Service Award: An author who goes above and beyond to support and unite others.
  • Creative Excellence Award: Consistently innovative creators setting new benchmarks on Episode.
  • Honorary Recognition: Authors whose quiet dedication behind the scenes strengthens the community.
  • Most Devoted Author Award: An author whose unwavering consistency and passion shine through every project.
Voting & Scoring 5

The Poll Vote runs on Instagram Stories. Stories or authors are grouped in fours, and voters pick their favourite from each group. Each story appears in three different polls throughout the round for equal exposure.

  • Follower vote: 8 points per vote
  • Non-follower vote: 2 points per vote

To maximise your points: encourage your readers to follow the @episode_buddies page before voting, this is the single most impactful thing you can do, as it converts their 2-point votes into 8-point ones.

The Feed Vote is scored based on engagement, likes, comments, and shares on the official @episode_buddies Instagram post featuring your story or author card.

  • Like from follower: 4 points · Like from non-follower: 1 point
  • Comment from follower: 8 points · Comment from non-follower: 2 points
  • Share (any user): 3 points

Comments are the highest-value action. Encourage your readers to leave genuine comments with the correct hashtag for voting and to share the post to reach new voters.

The S2S Vote is unique because voting for one story also benefits its paired partner. The Top 24 stories are paired into 12 duos, and when someone engages with a post to support their favourite, both stories in that pair receive the same points.

This means your result depends not just on your own fanbase, but also on who you're paired with. Stories paired with high-engagement partners benefit significantly, making S2S a round where cross-promotion and partnership awareness really matter.

Two pairs from the S2S Vote earn Fast Track spots directly into the Top 12.

The Head-to-Head Challenge is a direct, exclusive matchup. Unlike the S2S Vote where supporting one story also helps the other, in H2H a vote for one story or author is a vote against the other; the points are not shared.

Voting happens via Instagram feed comments (where voters state which story they support) and Instagram Story polls. Both are scored using the same weighted point system and combined for the final H2H result.

It is the most direct and intense voting round in the competition, the story or author with more community support survives, and the other may be eliminated.

For Instagram Story polls, Instagram only allows one vote per account per poll; so duplicate voting from the same account is not possible.

For feed vote events, each account can only like a post once. Multiple comments from the same account are not counted, only the first valid comment is scored. The same applies to the H2H Challenge comment vote.

The scoring system is designed to reward genuine, broad community support rather than mass repeat engagement from a small group.

Awards & Prizes 4

No. The Best Story Cover and Best Profile Picture rankings are completely separate from SOTY and AOTY scoring. They exist purely as awards to celebrate the visual creativity within the community - covers that instantly catch the eye and profile pictures that leave a lasting impression.

These awards are placed before the main competition events begin, almost like a warm-up to spotlight authors' incredible designs. Winning one earns you a Fast Track spot into the Top 24, but it does not add or remove points from your actual SOTY or AOTY score.

The SOTY winner receives:

  • 300 Gems - the top prize in the competition
  • A dedicated profile page on the Buddies WordPress website
  • One year of Instagram highlight showcase with story link
  • Two years of website showcase with story link
  • A screenshots post collage shared across the Buddies platforms
  • One year of ongoing story promotion on Buddies Stories, feed, and reels
  • Permanent induction into The Buddies Laureates Hall of Fame

Runners-up (1st through 3rd) also receive Gems, highlight showcases, website features, and promotion - scaled by placement.

The AOTY winner receives:

  • 300 Gems plus a surprise gift prize
  • A digital Winner Certificate
  • A dedicated profile page on the Buddies WordPress website
  • One year of Instagram highlight showcase
  • Two years of website showcase with profile link
  • One year of author promotion across Buddies platforms
  • Permanent induction into The Buddies Laureates — a lifetime honour

The Laureates title means the winner's name is permanently etched in Buddies history, with continued spotlighting of their work even in future seasons.

Special awards run alongside the main SOTY and AOTY competitions. Each winner receives 100 Gems, an Award Badge, and one year of Instagram highlight and website showcase.

SOTY Special Awards (judging committee): Best Directing, Best Interactivity, Best Plot, Best Story Cover (Judges' Choice), Best Dialogue & Characterization, Most Innovative Concept.

SOTY Community Awards: Most Popular Story (public website vote), Best Story Cover (Voters' Choice), Reader's Choice, Qualifier's Choice.

AOTY Special Awards: Best Instagram Aesthetics, Best Profile Picture (both Judges' and Community vote), Most Popular Author, Best Q&A Response, plus the six Special Honours (Rising Star, Lifetime Achievement, Community Service, Creative Excellence, Honorary Recognition, Most Devoted Author).

General 4

All competition updates, qualification announcements, voting posts, and result reveals are shared on the official @episode_buddies Instagram page. This is the primary platform for everything SOTY and AOTY, from the first nomination call in February right through to the winner announcement in December.

Following the page is also essential for voting, follower votes are worth significantly more than non-follower votes in every round. So following @episode_buddies isn't just about staying informed, it directly benefits the authors you want to support.
