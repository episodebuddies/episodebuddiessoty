Entry & Structure

To qualify for SOTY, a story must earn its place through one of the following pathways:

Enter a monthly Buddies contest and receive a score of 90 or higher.
Apply for a story review and receive a score of 90 or higher.
Receive a Team Nomination from any member of the Buddies team.
Apply through the Special Opportunity Slot when applications open.
Receive an Honorable Mention from any Buddies team member.
The 500,000 read cap applies; stories exceeding this limit at the time of the competition opening are not eligible to compete.

Before the contest begins, all qualified stories enter an online vote on the official Buddies website. The story with the most votes automatically advances to the semifinals (Top 12), regardless of their Stage 1 score.

Stage 1 includes four events: the Judges' Evaluation (4 categories: Directing, Interactivity, Plot, Cover), the 1st Feed Vote (stories in groups of 8, scored by likes/comments/shares), the 1st Poll Vote (different groups, Instagram Stories), and the Author's Vote (each contestant votes for 3 other stories, ranked 1st/2nd/3rd). Top performers in each event earn points toward the Top 24.

The Top 24 are revealed — 20 from Stage 1 scores plus 4 Fast Track spots (Best Story Cover, Best Directing, Best Interactivity, Best Plot). A Save-2-Stories Vote and a 2nd Poll Vote determine which 12 advance, alongside a Most Popular Story vote granting an additional Fast Track to the Top 12.

The Top 12 are announced. Stories are paired into 6 head-to-head matchups. Unlike S2S, each vote is exclusive - supporting one story does not help the other. Voting takes place via feed comments and Instagram Story polls simultaneously. The Top 8 advance.

The Top 8 are announced. A final Feed Vote and a final Poll Vote run simultaneously. The Top 4 are revealed before the overall winner and all special award winners are announced on December 30.

Entry & Structure

To qualify for AOTY, your story must be entered into the 2026 Story of the Year competition. Every SOTY qualifier is automatically eligible to compete in AOTY - no separate application is needed.

AOTY evaluates the author as a whole; their Q&A responses, Instagram presence, community impact, and dedication, not just the story. The title is a lifetime honour; past winners cannot re-enter.

All 40 authors enter an online website poll. The public votes for their favourite author. The author with the most votes automatically advances to the Top 12, skipping all earlier stages.

Stage 1 includes four events: the Judges' Evaluation (4 categories: Q&A Round, Aesthetics, PFP, Impact Message), the 1st Feed Vote (authors in groups of 8, scored by likes/comments/shares), the 1st Poll Vote (different groups, Instagram Stories), and the Author's Vote (each contestant votes for 3 other authors, ranked 1st/2nd/3rd). Top performers in each event earn points toward the Top 24.

The Top 24 are announced — 20 from Stage 1 scores plus 4 Fast Track spots (Best PFP, Most Devoted Author, Best Aesthetics, Best Impact Message). A 2nd Poll Vote runs to determine which 12 advance, alongside the Most Popular Author & Best Q&A Round vote which grants an additional Fast Track to the Top 12.

The Top 12 are announced. Authors are paired into 6 head-to-head matchups. Each author answers an impactful question asked by the team which will be shared with their fans and the Episode community. The community votes via comments on the feed post, and the judging panel separately scores each message on impact, clarity, originality, and community value. Combined scores determine which 8 advance.

The Top 8 are announced. Each remaining author answers one final question in a private interview. Scores from the final interview are added to cumulative totals. The Top 4 are revealed before the overall winner and all special award winners are announced.
