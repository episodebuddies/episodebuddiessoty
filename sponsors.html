Sponsor Benefits

What you receive as a sponsor

We believe sponsorship should work both ways. Every partner who stands behind our competitions receives meaningful, visible recognition throughout the full season.

🎨
Recognition Across Event Graphics & Announcements
Your name or handle is included in our official event graphics and announcements — posters, trailers, and promotional materials — from the season opener right through to the finale reveal.
📌
Mentions in Public Posts & Updates
You are named in our public appreciation posts and event updates throughout the competition, keeping your account in front of the community at every key moment of the season.
🎬
Recognition at Award Reveals & Finale Events
Your contribution is acknowledged during award reveals and our finale events — the most-watched moments of the entire competition, when the community is at peak engagement.
🌐
Permanent Sponsor Page Listing
Every sponsor receives a dedicated card on this page, including your name, handle, and what you contributed. This page remains live and linked from our navigation beyond the season end.
📅
Priority Visibility in Seasonal Campaigns
During our countdowns, voting pushes, and campaign bursts — the busiest windows in our competition calendar — sponsors receive priority placement in our content and communications.
📖
Included in Credits, Hall of Fame & Recap Publications
Your name appears in our season credits, Hall of Fame posts, and any recap publications we produce. Your support is a permanent part of that season's official record.

What sponsors can contribute as prizes

Sponsorship is flexible — there are no tiers and no minimum. You contribute what you can, in whatever form makes sense for you. These are the three types of prize contributions we welcome.

💎
Gems

Episode gems are the most universally valued prize on the platform. Gem contributions go directly into official winner and finalist prize packages and are one of the most tangible ways to reward competing authors.

Winner prize gems Finalist rewards Top 8 packages
🎨
Art

Original artwork — character illustrations, story covers, splash art, or any custom creative work — makes for a deeply personal prize. Particularly valued by authors who want something unique tied to their story or identity.

Character art Story covers Splash art Custom illustrations
✏️
Edits

Graphic edits — promotional graphics, cover edits, announcement cards, or styled author features — are creative and visible. They benefit the recipient directly and elevate the presentation of the competition as a whole.

Promo graphics Cover edits Author features Announcement cards

Prize type and scale are agreed between you and the Buddies Team before the season begins. There are no tiers — every sponsor is equally valued and recognised regardless of what they contribute.
