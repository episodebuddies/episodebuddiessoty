Story of the Year

The History of SOTY

The Story of the Year (SOTY) competition was first introduced in 2022 as part of the Buddies Support Hub's mission to uplift and spotlight exceptional writers in the Episode community. What started as a simple contest open to stories earning review scores of 90 or above and winners of monthly contests, quickly evolved into something much bigger.

The inaugural season brought together over 20 captivating stories, each showcasing unique creativity, powerful storytelling, and immersive directing. From emotional rollercoasters to thrilling adventures, these stories raised the bar for what community writing could achieve.

Over the years, SOTY has grown into a signature annual event, drawing participation and excitement from authors and readers across the globe. It is more than a competition; it is a movement that celebrates excellence and innovation in storytelling while giving authors a platform to be recognized beyond their immediate circles.

Today SOTY runs across multiple stages over three months, with over 38 qualifying stories competing through community voting events, judge evaluations, and head-to-head battles; all culminating in a single winner inducted permanently into The Buddies Laureates Hall of Fame.

The Vision Behind SOTY

Discover, honor, and celebrate the best creative storytelling within the Episode community each year.
Highlight remarkable stories and authors who pour their passion into their craft.
Encourage innovation and quality, inspiring writers to push their creative limits.
Build a supportive community where every author feels seen, valued, and celebrated.
Create a yearly tradition that brings authors, readers, and supporters together in celebration of storytelling.

Author of the Year

The History of AOTY

The Author of the Year (AOTY) competition was first introduced in 2023 as part of the grand finale of the Story of the Year event. The goal was simple: to celebrate the authors whose dedication, creativity, and community impact stood out throughout the year.

In its debut year, Fern emerged as the deserving inaugural winner, recognized not just for exceptional storytelling with Symphony, but for her influence and contributions to the Episode community as a whole.

Following its success, the organizers gave AOTY its own dedicated platform in 2024. A special planning committee was formed to craft every aspect of the competition, introducing engaging audience events, creative Q&A rounds, and interactive challenges that made AOTY an immersive experience far beyond just an award ceremony.

Unlike SOTY, the AOTY title is a lifetime honour. Past winners are permanently retired from future competitions, ensuring the award always goes to someone new and continuing to grow the legacy of Buddies Laureates year by year.

The Vision Behind AOTY

Honor the people behind the stories; the creators whose passion drives the community forward.
Recognize and reward authors who go above and beyond through outstanding writing, directing, or community support.
Inspire new and existing authors by showcasing creative journeys worth celebrating.
Foster unity and motivation, proving that authorship is about community, creativity, and impact.
Build a permanent legacy of Laureates that the Episode community can look to for inspiration year after year.

How It Works

Two competitions. One season.

Both competitions run October through December each year. Here is how each one works, side by side.

Story of the Year — SOTY
Celebrating the best story
01
Popularity Vote All qualifying stories enter a public vote before the competition begins. The most popular story advances straight to the Top 12 semifinals.
02
Stage 1 — Full Field (38 → 24) Author's Interview, 1st Feed Vote, 1st Poll Vote, and Author's Vote all run simultaneously. Stories earn points and compete for the Top 24 spots.
03
Stage 2 — Top 24 (24 → 12) Save-2-Stories Vote runs in pairs, votes for one story count for both. Two Fast Track pairs advance automatically. A 2nd Poll Vote determines the remaining Top 12.
04
Stage 3 — Semifinals (12 → 8) Head-to-Head Challenge. Stories face off in exclusive pairs, votes for one do NOT help the other. Feed comments and Story polls combine for scores.
05
Stage 4 — Finals (8 → Winner) Final Feed Vote, Top 4 announcement, then the Winner - inducted permanently into the Buddies Laureates Hall of Fame.
Author of the Year — AOTY
Celebrating the person behind it
01
Popularity Vote All 40 authors enter a public website vote. The most popular author advances straight to the Top 12, skipping Stage 1.
02
Stage 1 — Full Field (38 → 24) Feed Vote, Poll Vote, Q&A Round (judge-scored privately), and Best Aesthetics Contest all run in Stage 1. Combined scores determine the Top 24.
03
Stage 2 — Top 24 (24 → 12) A 2nd Poll Vote and the Most Popular Author community vote both run. The Most Popular Author winner earns a Fast Track straight to the Top 12.
04
Stage 3 — Semifinals (12 → 8) Head-to-Head Challenge - authors write impactful messages for their fans. Community votes and judge scores combined determine the Top 8.
05
Stage 4 — Finals (8 → Winner) Final interview round, Final Poll Vote, Top 4 announcement, then the Winner - awarded a lifetime AOTY title. Past winners permanently retired.
Qualification

How to qualify for 2026.

Qualification for SOTY 2026 opens in February and closes at the end of September. There are four ways in for SOTY, and AOTY qualification is automatic for all SOTY entrants.

01
Monthly Contest Score
February – September
Enter any Buddies monthly contest between February and September. Score 90 or above and your story automatically qualifies for SOTY.
02
Story Review Score
Available year-round
Apply for a Story Review from the Buddies Review Team. Receive a score of 90 or above and your story earns automatic SOTY qualification.
03
Special Opportunity Slot
Limited applications
Apply directly for a Special Opportunity Slot when applications open. Watch our Instagram page for the announcement. Places are limited.
04
Honorable Mention
Team nomination
Receive an Honorable Mention or Team Nomination from any member of the Buddies Support Hub team. This is awarded at the team's discretion.

AOTY Qualification: Every story that qualifies for SOTY is automatically eligible for AOTY — no separate application needed. The read cap for SOTY is 250,000 reads at the time of the competition opening. Stories above this threshold are not eligible to compete.
